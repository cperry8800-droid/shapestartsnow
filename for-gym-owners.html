How It Works

Revenue

How you earn with Shape Pass

You keep the majority of every visit

When a Shape Pass member checks into your facility, you earn a share of their monthly subscription based on visit frequency. The more they train at your gym, the more you earn. Shape handles all billing, member support, and payment processing.

There are no upfront costs, no monthly platform fees, and no hidden charges. You only earn — never pay.
