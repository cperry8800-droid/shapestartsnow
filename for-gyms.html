How It Works

Three steps to get your facility on Shape

List Your Facility

Create your gym profile with location, amenities, photos, and class schedules. Shape makes your facility discoverable to members in your area.

Onboard Your Team

Invite your trainers and nutritionists to join under your facility. They build their own profiles while representing your brand on the platform.

Start Earning

Sell memberships, class packs, and day passes directly through Shape. Members subscribe, you get paid. Shape handles the rest.
