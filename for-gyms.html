Shape Pass

One pass. Shape access.

The Shape Pass gives you access to hundreds of partner gyms and studios across the country. Walk into any Shape-partnered facility, check in with the app, and train.

How It Works

Three steps to train in the Shape network

Get the Shape Pass

Subscribe to the Shape Pass — one monthly fee gives you access to our entire network of partner facilities nationwide.

Find a Gym Near You

Browse the Shape Pass marketplace to find partner facilities in your area. Filter by type, amenities, classes, and distance.

Check In and Train

Walk into any partner gym, check in through the Shape app, and start training. No day passes, no contracts, no hassle.

Access Everywhere

Gyms, studios, and training spaces across the country

The Shape Pass covers a wide range of facilities so you always have a place to train — whether you're at home, traveling, or just want to try something new.

Traditional Gyms

Full-service facilities with free weights, machines, cardio, and group classes. Walk in with your Shape membership and get to work.

Boutique Studios

Access specialty studios that normally require separate memberships — all included with the Shape Pass.

CrossFit Boxes

Drop into CrossFit affiliates across the country. Join the WOD, meet the community, and train with experienced coaches wherever you are.
