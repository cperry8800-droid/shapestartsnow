About Shape

Fitness on your terms

Shape is the platform that connects you with expert trainers and nutritionists — no long-term contracts, no hidden fees, no gatekeeping. Just real pros, real results, and a community that keeps you going.

Our Mission

We believe great training shouldn't be a luxury

Traditional personal training costs $60-150 per session. That prices out most people from ever working with a qualified professional. Shape changes that by letting trainers and nutritionists set their own subscription prices, creating options for every budget.

Whether you want a $15/month bodyweight program you can do at home or a $100/month premium coaching experience with daily check-ins, Shape gives you the freedom to choose what works for your life, your goals, and your wallet.

No algorithms deciding what you see. No corporate workout templates. Just real professionals building real programs for real people.

How It Works

Getting started is simple

Sign up for Shape — $10/month

Your membership includes full platform access, AI Trainer, progress tracking, nutrition scheduling, community chat, and Shape Radio with ad-free workout music.

Browse trainers and nutritionists

Explore profiles, read reviews, preview workouts, and find the pros that match your goals. Filter by specialty — strength, HIIT, cardio, functional, at home, and more.

Subscribe or buy individual plans

Each pro sets their own pricing. Subscribe monthly for ongoing access to their full library, or purchase individual workout and meal plans one at a time.

Train, track, and transform

Follow your workouts, log your progress, track your nutrition, message your trainer directly, and connect with the community. Everything lives in one place.

Switch it up whenever you want

Cancel a trainer subscription, try someone new, mix and match — Shape is built for flexibility. Your fitness journey evolves, and your platform should too.
